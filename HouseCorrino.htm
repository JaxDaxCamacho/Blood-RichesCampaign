SECONDARY DOMAINS

Several minor domains are either directly controlled by the House or managed on their behalf by another House who tithe a portion of the profits. This is often referred to as the ‘Lion’s Share’, as it is often larger than it might usually be

Military Power

House Corrino controls the Sardaukar, who are the most able soldiers in the Known Universe. The Landsraad believe it is impossible to beat the Sardaukar on the field of battle unless all the other Houses join together against Corrino — and there is little prospect of that happening. Although dependent on the Guild for transportation, no one doubts House Corrino holds military superiority over all other Houses in the Imperium.
